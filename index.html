Zamora, Michoacan, Mexico

  • About Zamora

    • Location

    Zamora is a city located in the state of Michoacan, Mexico. It is located approximately 100 miles west of Mexico City.

    About

    History

    Zamora was founded in 1574 by the Spanish conquerors. It played an important role during the Mexican War of Independence and was officially recognized as a city in 1825.

    Culture

    Zamora is known for its rich cultural heritage, including its traditional music, dance, and cuisine. It is home to several museums and galleries, including the Museum of Regional History and the Fine Arts Museum.

    Industry

    Zamora is an important center for agriculture, particularly the production of strawberries, peaches, and avocados. It is also known for its furniture and handicraft industries.
